Friends

Grondo Copperback

Race: Hafling
Age: 25
Location: Hammerfel

Personality:
The halfling is a very charismatic fella. He will always have a smile on his face, and be helpful and kind, he believes that if you do good for others, at some point, others will do good for you too. If met during his off time, Grondo enjoys time alone, to read or go on tranquil walks just outside the walls. He will still have a short conversation, and will then politely excuse themselves to enjoy some time alone.

Aspirations:
He dreams of leaving Hammerfel and opening his own inn in Emberfalls.

Physical description:
He has slightly short brown wavy hair and hazel eyes. His skin is slightly tan with a few freckles spread across his nose and cheeks. he's around 90cm tall and has a sturdy build of someone who does some heavy lifting. He has slight bags under his eyes and his nails painted the similar dark purple of his eye shadows. He usually wears a navy blue apron and white T-shirt and linen trousers. Around his neck he carries a dark copper key on a grey string.

Quirks:
When nervous he tends to fiddle with the key he carries on his neck.

Grinda Copperback

Race: Hafling
Age: 140
Location: Hammerfel

Personality:
Despite her diminutive stature, she exudes a quiet confidence and a no-nonsense attitude. Resourceful and pragmatic, she approaches challenges with a level-headed determination, never backing down from adversity. While she may seem reserved at first glance, she possesses a sharp wit and a dry sense of humour, often catching others off guard with her unexpected quips.

Aspirations:
Unknown

Physical description:
She possesses curly brown hair that is usually tied up, complemented by dark brown eyes that hold a steady gaze. Standing at 85cm tall, her stature is modest yet sturdy. Her hands, bearing the marks of manual labour, speak to a lifetime of hard work. Her skin carries a dark tan. A tattoo of a sword adorns her back, with the hilt visible at the nape of her neck. During work, she wears practical attire: thick black boots, baggy hide trousers, and a white linen top

Quirks:
Unknown
