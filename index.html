Welcome to Daedrion

The largest continent on Grathos, Daedrion, renowned for its astounding biome diversity and harrowing wilds, is named after the third King of Elm Deep, Daedrion Elmsveil. Widely believed to be the testing ground for the gods, this enigmatic land teems with monstrous beasts, fiery storms, and magic rarer than mithril. The dangers of Daedrion have made it difficult to establish settlements, with the few existing ones located in areas cleared of most threats or where creatures are manageable with adequate guard protection. This untamed expanse promises both the allure of adventure and the constant threat of the unknown, offering a perilous yet enticing frontier for the brave. So Adventurers, welcome to Daedrion!
