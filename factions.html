Factions

Emberfalls Council

Brief Summary: Emberfalls Council is the leading power within Daedrion. Located in the capital of Emberfalls this faction has its hands in every major settlment. From controlling armies to granting permits, this burochratic nightmare is doing it.

Mythorium Miners Co.

Brief Summary: A powerful and ruthless mining corporation known for their monopolistic control over rare gemstone deposits. They are responsible for the foundation of 3 settlments within the continent, Phandalin, Hammerfel & Hilltop Hollow.
